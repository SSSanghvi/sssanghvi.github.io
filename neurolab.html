Neurolab Logo

I worked with the people over at NeuroLab, a student organization at UC Berkeley, to create a new logo in March 2018. The logo is analogous to the club's mixed focus between Neurology and Electrical Engineering. In order to illustrate how the club brings these two fields together, I came up with a design where the two lobes of a brain are connected with a capacitor - which can be interpreted as providing power to the mind. ​
