Foreword

This is a web book. About me.

why is your website a book, sahil?

I think there's something beautiful about the way books are organized. I'd rather have my website organized in the same way, with chapters and pages instead of tabs and links. It's more fun this way, and I think it's easier to read as well. I'm also a bit of a typography nerd, and seeing a beautiful book (whether it's online or physical) makes me really happy. This one is made using Pollen Pollen Docs and Tufte Tufte Repository CSS.

I hope you enjoy it!
